My Goal

My goal is to grow as a computer science student by gaining hands-on experience, developing strong technical skills, and exploring areas such as AI, data science, and cybersecurity. I’m especially interested in how technology can be used to solve real-world problems and make systems more efficient and secure. I’m currently a Student Ambassador at the University of the West of Scotland, where I enjoy supporting other students and representing the university community. I’m also part of the Dell Technologies STEMAspire Mentoring Programme, which helps me connect with industry professionals and better understand the opportunities in tech. For me, it’s not just about coding — it’s about learning, experimenting, and building a foundation that will prepare me for a future career in technology.
